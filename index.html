06 / questions

Things people ask before signing up.

Do my candidates know they're talking to AI?

Yes. FlexForce identifies itself at the start of every call. 83%+ of candidates rate the AI screen positively because it's fast, never judgmental, and respects their time. Many forget partway through that they're not talking to a human. If anyone asks for a human callback, you get pinged immediately.

How fast can I be live?

30 minutes after our onboarding call. You give me your trade, state, salary range, and the 3–5 questions you usually ask. I wire up your branded apply page (apply.flexforce.ai/yourshop), publish your phone number, and you're live the same day.

What if FlexForce isn't a fit for my shop?

The first 30 days are completely free — no card on file. If you don't get at least one solid hire from FlexForce in your first 30 days, you owe nothing and we part friends. After 30 days, your plan starts at $299/mo and you can cancel anytime.

Does it integrate with my existing software (ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro)?

Yes. Growth plan ($599/mo) includes Jobber + Housecall Pro integration today. ServiceTitan integration is on Premium ($999/mo). Hired candidates flow straight into your existing dispatch software — no double entry.

How does FlexForce handle Spanish-speaking applicants?

Natively. The AI screens in fluent Spanish (and 6 other languages on the Growth plan). For foreign-trained techs whose licenses don't transfer cleanly, FlexForce flags them as "credential bridge candidates" so you can decide whether to hire as a helper while they re-credential — without losing the talent to a competitor.

Is this EEOC-compliant? What about AI hiring laws?

Built for compliance from day one. I follow NYC AEDT, California AB-2930, and federal EEOC frameworks. The AI never makes the final hiring decision — you do. Quarterly bias-audit reports are included on the Premium plan. Candidates are always informed they're talking to AI.

Who's behind FlexForce?

FlexForce is built by Jack — a solo founder who got tired of watching small contractors lose great hires to broken software. When you email support, you get me directly. No team to escalate to. No sales rep to schedule around. That's the deal: smaller company, sharper service.
