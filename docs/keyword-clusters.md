## How to read this doc

- **Primary keyword** — the exact phrase the page is optimized for (in H1, title, first 100 words, meta)
- **Secondary keywords** — related terms woven naturally into the body
- **Intent** — what the searcher actually wants (BOFU = buy/act, MOFU = learn/compare, TOFU = research)
- **Competition** — estimated organic competition (Low / Medium / High)
- **Wave** — which staggered commit ships this page

## Intent notes by pattern

**BOFU money pages:** searcher is a small contractor actively looking to solve a hiring problem RIGHT NOW.
Target: "hire [trade] [state]" — commercial intent, usually under 100 monthly searches per geo
but converts at 5–15% when page is locally specific and CTA is strong.

**MOFU hubs + guides:** searcher is researching. They know they have a hiring problem but haven't
committed to a solution. Goal: capture email or trial via the guide's CTA.
Target: "how to hire [trade]", "cost to hire [trade]", "why applicants ghost" — 200–2000 monthly searches each.

**Comparison BOFU:** searcher is evaluating FlexForce specifically vs a known alternative.
These should rank within 4 weeks because competition is low (no one optimizes for
"FlexForce vs Indeed" yet). Convert at very high rates.

**Report TOFU:** citable artifact. Goal is AI citation, backlinks, email captures. Not expected
to drive direct signups — expected to drive credibility that helps the rest of the site rank.
